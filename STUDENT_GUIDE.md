## üé≠ Emotion Tracking (What You Need to Know)

### How It Works:
- Every 60 seconds during meeting
- Face-API detects your facial expressions
- Tracks 7 emotions: Happy, Sad, Angry, Surprised, Fearful, Disgusted, Neutral
- Measures your attentiveness level
- No photos or videos saved (just emotion data)

### Why It Matters:
- Helps lecturer understand class engagement
- Identifies when students are confused
- Improves teaching methods
- Part of your participation grade

### What You'll See:
- Small indicator when emotion detected
- "Face detected" message in console (if you check)
- No visible scores or alerts on your screen

### Lecturer Sees:
- Overall class mood
- Individual engagement levels
- When students seem confused or disengaged
- Alerts for low attentiveness

### Tips for Best Results:
- Face the camera directly
- Ensure good lighting
- Stay visible in camera frame
- Stay engaged and focused

## üìä Attendance Tracking

### How Attendance Works:

**Automatic Recording:**
1. When you join meeting ‚Üí Record join time
2. When you leave meeting ‚Üí Record leave time
3. If you rejoin ‚Üí New session recorded
4. Total duration calculated automatically

### Attendance Status:

**Based on Meeting Duration:**
- **Present**: Attended >75% of meeting
- **Partial**: Attended 50-75% of meeting
- **Absent**: Attended <50% of meeting
- **Late**: Joined >5 minutes after start

### View Your Attendance:
1. Go to **My Attendance** or subject details
2. See all meetings with:
   - Meeting date
   - Join time
   - Leave time
   - Duration attended
   - Attendance percentage
   - Status

### Multiple Sessions:
If you leave and rejoin:
- Session 1: First join ‚Üí first leave
- Session 2: Second join ‚Üí second leave
- Total duration = Sum of all sessions

### Attendance Tips:
- Join on time (within 5 minutes)
- Stay for full meeting duration
- If internet drops, rejoin quickly
- Maintain >75% attendance for full credit

## üìà Progress Tracking

### Your Level System:

**Current Level:**
- View on dashboard and subject pages
- Based on assignment scores and attendance
- Unlocks extra learning modules

**Level Progression:**
- **Level 1**: New students, basic content
- **Level 2**: Average performance, extra modules
- **Level 3**: High performance, advanced content

### How Levels Are Calculated:
1. **Assignment Grades**: 60% weight
   - Average score across all assignments
2. **Attendance**: 25% weight
   - Meeting participation percentage
3. **Engagement**: 15% weight
   - Emotion tracking attentiveness

### Checking Your Progress:
1. Dashboard shows overall level
2. Each subject shows subject-specific level
3. View detailed breakdown in **Progress** page

## üö® Troubleshooting

### Can't Login:
1. Check email spelling
2. Verify password (case-sensitive)
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try different browser
5. Contact administrator

### Can't Join Meeting:
1. Check meeting start time
2. Click "Join" button (appears 5 min before)
3. Allow camera/microphone permissions
4. Refresh page and try again
5. Check internet connection

### Camera Not Working:
1. Check browser permissions
2. Close other apps using camera
3. Try different browser
4. Restart computer
5. Check camera privacy settings (Windows)

### Assignment Upload Fails:
1. Check file size (max limit)
2. Verify file format (PDF usually required)
3. Check internet connection
4. Try different file
5. Contact lecturer if issue persists

### Emotion Tracking Not Working:
- Ensure camera is enabled
- Face the camera directly
- Improve lighting
- No need to worry - lecturer will see overall class data

### Attendance Not Showing:
- Wait a few minutes after meeting
- Refresh the page
- Check you were in meeting >5 minutes
- Contact lecturer if still missing

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I submit assignments after the deadline?**
A: Yes, but may have point deductions. Check with lecturer.

**Q: What if I miss a meeting?**
A: Your attendance is marked absent. Contact lecturer if valid reason.

**Q: How often is emotion tracking done?**
A: Every 60 seconds during meetings (configurable by admin).

**Q: Can I see my emotion data?**
A: Currently only lecturers see detailed data. You see your engagement level.

**Q: How do I unlock extra modules?**
A: Achieve Level 2 or 3 through good grades and attendance.

**Q: What if my internet drops during meeting?**
A: Rejoin as soon as possible. Multiple sessions are recorded.

**Q: Can I delete a submitted assignment?**
A: No, contact lecturer to resubmit or update.

**Q: How is my level calculated?**
A: Based on assignment grades (60%), attendance (25%), engagement (15%).

**Q: What browser should I use?**
A: Chrome or Edge recommended for best compatibility.

**Q: Is my face video recorded?**
A: No, only emotion data is analyzed and stored, not video/images.
